Bio.

I'm Malaysia, a creative mind who fell in love with how to technology can look beautiful. Web design and development speak to me because they blend logic and artistry. I’m passionate about creating digital spaces that feel warm, expressive, and alive. To me, web tech is the bridge between imagination and interaction. As a future software developer, I want to design websites and apps that not only work but also feel like me: detailed, meaningful, and made with love

Pink Tiger Lily

The pink Tiger Lily represents confidence and compassion, two things I try to balance every day. It’s bold without being harsh, and that’s how I want to move through life. I love how each petal stretches open like it’s unafraid to take up space, reminding me to do the same. Fun fact: Tiger Lilies are known to symbolize wealth and pride in Asian cultures, but to me, they stand for growth and unapologetic beauty.

Kali Uchis

Kali Uchis inspires me because she lives in her femininity without apology. Her music and visuals celebrate self-love, transformation, and freedom, values that resonate deeply with me. I want to walk through life with that same elegance and ease, grounded in who I am, glowing in my own energy.

Light Pink

Pink is more than just a color, it’s a feeling. To me, light pink represents calm, care, and quiet confidence. It’s a color that speaks to softness but holds strength underneath, much like femininity itself. Every shade of pink reminds me that beauty can exist in gentleness and that vulnerability doesn’t make you weak, it makes you real. Whether we admit it or not, pink has shaped the way we all see love, comfort, and warmth.
