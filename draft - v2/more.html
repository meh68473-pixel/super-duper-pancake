Spider-Man’s Co-Creators & Artists

Steve Ditko

Steve Ditko designed Spider-Man’s iconic costume and illustrated his earliest comic book adventures, defining his visual world.

Jack Kirby

Jack Kirby worked with Stan Lee on early concepts of Spider-Man. Although his design wasn't used, his contributions guided the character's direction.

John Romita Sr.

John Romita Sr. took over after Ditko and gave Spider-Man a polished, expressive style that shaped comics for decades.
